Buying Motorcycles
How do I find the right motorcycle for me?

We recommend considering your riding experience, intended use (commuting, touring, off-road), and budget. Use our advanced search filters to narrow down options by make, model, year, price range, and features. Our team is also available for personalized recommendations.

Can I test drive a motorcycle before purchasing?

Yes, test drives are available for most motorcycles at our physical locations. For online purchases, we offer a 48-hour inspection period after delivery. During this time, you can thoroughly inspect and test the motorcycle to ensure it meets your expectations.

What documents should I check before buying a used motorcycle?

Always verify the following documents:

  • Original logbook showing ownership transfer history
  • Valid insurance certificate
  • Current inspection certificate (if applicable)
  • Service history records
  • Clearance certificate from relevant authorities
  • Seller's identification document
Do you offer warranties on used motorcycles?

Yes, all our certified pre-owned motorcycles come with a 6-month limited warranty that covers major mechanical components. The warranty details are provided with each motorcycle listing. For motorcycles sold "as-is", we clearly indicate this in the description.

Selling Motorcycles
How do I sell my motorcycle through your platform?

Selling your motorcycle is simple:

  1. Create a seller account on our platform
  2. Provide details about your motorcycle (make, model, year, condition)
  3. Upload clear photos from multiple angles
  4. Set your asking price based on our valuation tool
  5. Schedule an inspection (optional but recommended)
  6. Once approved, your listing goes live
  7. Communicate with potential buyers through our secure messaging
What fees are involved when selling my motorcycle?

We charge a success fee of 5% of the final sale price, which includes:

  • Professional photography
  • Featured listing placement
  • Secure payment processing
  • Documentation assistance
  • Marketing across our partner networks

There are no upfront fees to list your motorcycle.

How long does it typically take to sell a motorcycle?

The selling time varies based on:

  • Make and model popularity
  • Pricing compared to market value
  • Seasonal demand
  • Quality of photos and description

On average, well-priced motorcycles in good condition sell within 7-14 days. Our premium listings typically sell 30% faster.

Can I sell a motorcycle that still has financing?

Yes, you can sell a motorcycle with outstanding financing, but the process requires additional steps:

  1. Obtain a settlement letter from your financier
  2. Disclose the outstanding amount to potential buyers
  3. At sale completion, we'll coordinate with your financier to pay off the loan
  4. Any surplus funds will be transferred to you
  5. The buyer receives a clear title once financing is settled
Payments & Financing
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple secure payment methods:

  • Bank transfers (most common)
  • Mobile money payments (M-Pesa, Airtel Money)
  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Cash payments at our verified locations
  • Escrow services for high-value transactions
Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with leading financial institutions to offer competitive financing options:

  • Loan amounts from Ksh 50,000 to Ksh 2,000,000
  • Flexible repayment periods (6-48 months)
  • Interest rates starting at 12% per annum
  • Quick approval process (24-48 hours)
  • Both salaried and self-employed individuals eligible

Apply through our platform with minimal documentation.

How does your escrow service work?

Our escrow service protects both buyers and sellers:

  1. Buyer deposits payment into our secure escrow account
  2. Seller is notified and prepares motorcycle for delivery
  3. Buyer receives and inspects the motorcycle
  4. Once satisfied, buyer approves the release of funds
  5. Payment is transferred to seller within 24 hours
  6. If not satisfied, buyer initiates return process

We charge a small fee of 1.5% for this service (min Ksh 1,500).

Are there any hidden fees when purchasing?

We believe in transparent pricing. The listed price includes:

  • Our service fee
  • Basic documentation processing
  • Pre-sale inspection

Additional costs may include:

  • Delivery charges (if applicable)
  • Optional extended warranty
  • Financing processing fees
  • Government transfer fees (typically 3-5% of value)

All additional costs are clearly disclosed before you commit to purchase.

Delivery & Inspection
How does motorcycle delivery work?

We offer nationwide delivery through our trusted partners:

  1. After purchase confirmation, we coordinate delivery schedule
  2. Motorcycle is securely loaded onto specialized transport
  3. You receive tracking information and driver contact
  4. Delivery typically takes 1-5 business days depending on distance
  5. Upon arrival, you inspect and sign for receipt

Delivery fees range from Ksh 2,000 to Ksh 15,000 based on distance and motorcycle size.

What happens if I'm not satisfied after delivery?

We offer a 48-hour satisfaction guarantee:

  • If the motorcycle doesn't match the listing description
  • If undisclosed mechanical issues are found
  • If you change your mind (restocking fee applies)

Simply contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery to initiate a return. We'll arrange pickup and process your refund within 3 business days.

How do I transfer ownership after purchase?

We handle the entire ownership transfer process:

  1. Seller provides signed transfer documents
  2. We verify all documentation
  3. Submit transfer application to relevant authorities
  4. Pay applicable government transfer fees
  5. New logbook is processed within 7-10 business days
  6. Digital copy of new logbook sent immediately
  7. Physical copy mailed to your address

Our transfer service fee is Ksh 2,500 which includes all processing and follow-up.
